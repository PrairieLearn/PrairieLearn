Timed exams expire based on both a relative cutoff, timeLimitMin, and absolute cutoff, endDate. Depending on when a student begins the exam, these two settings can interact to shorten the exam's default timer.

If a student begins the exam close enough to endDate such that the remaining time to endDate is less than timeLimitMin:

  1. The student's exam timer will be initialized with the abbreviated time to endDate, not the full timeLimitMin (Figure 1).
  2. The student's exam timer will then be reduced by up to 60 seconds to properly sync with the server. In practice, this means the student may lose up to 60 additional seconds by beginning the exam too close to endDate.

See the documentation on documentation on time limits and documentation on timed exams for details.
