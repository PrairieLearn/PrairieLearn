For classes with many students, the Gradebook and Students pages have been dramatically improved by Jonatan Schroeder, with input from James Yu (University of British Columbia).

When you scroll these pages, the header row and student ID column are now pinned and always visible. This makes the Gradebook so much better with huge classes and many assessments! There are also many other new features that help with large classes, including built-in search, toggleable column visibility, and auto-refresh so that you can see scores changing as students are working on assessments.

Try out the new pages by switching to "Instructor view" in any course and going to either:

  • "Gradebook" in the top menubar
  • "Assessments" → choose assessment → "Students" tab
