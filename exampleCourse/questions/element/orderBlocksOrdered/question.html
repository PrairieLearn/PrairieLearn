This question illustrates the grading-method="ordered" mode of pl-order-blocks.

Drag all the prime numbers below to the answer zone in ascending order.

This example uses the attributes min-incorrect="2" and max-incorrect="4" to display a minimum of 2 incorrect distractors and maximum of 4. The attribute source-blocks-order="random" shuffles the blocks in the source area. The default grading-method="ordered" requires that the correct answer follows the order defined by the pl-answers.
