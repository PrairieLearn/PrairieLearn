This question demonstrates the image capture element, which enables students to submit images to questions using their local camera or an external device (e.g. a phone or tablet).

Image capture with mobile capture enabled:

Consider the function $f(x) = arcsin(2x) + cos(4x^3-5x)+e^{x^3}$. Find $f'(x)$ and show your work.

Image capture with mobile capture disabled:

Sketch the bounded region enclosed by the curves $y=-x^2+3$ and $y=2x^2$.
