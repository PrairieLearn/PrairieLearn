The purpose of this experiment is to determine the convective heat transfer coefficients for natural convection over pin fins.

Seven thermocouples are embedded along the length of the fin. One thermocouple is placed at the base of the fin, which is the reference position $x_0 = 0$. We denote the temperature at the base as $T_b = T(x_0)$. The other thermocouples are placed at positions $x_1$, $x_2$, ... with corresponsing temperatures $T_1$, $T_2$, etc.

The pin fin has diameter $D$, length $L$ and is made of stainless steel with thermal conductivity $k = {{params.k}} \rm W/mK$.
