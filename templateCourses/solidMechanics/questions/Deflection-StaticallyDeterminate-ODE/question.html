The cantilever beam has length $2L$ where $L = {{params.L}} \rm\ m$, elasticity modulus $E = {{params.E}} \rm\ GPa$, and cross-section with moment of inertia $I = {{params.Iz}} \times 10^{-6} \rm\ m^4$. A force $P = {{params.P}} \rm\ kN$ and a couple moment $M = {{params.M}} \rm\ kN.m$ are applied at midpoint as indicated below.

P M L E Iz

Obtain an expression for the deflection curve $y(x)$ in each of the intervals below. You need to write the numeric coefficients of the equations. Note that $x$ is given in ${\rm\ meters \ [m]}$.

$0 \lt x \lt L $:

$y_1(x)$ = + $x$ + $x^2$ + $x^3$ + $x^4 \rm\ [m]$

$L \lt x \lt 2L $:

$y_2(x)$ = + $x$ + $x^2$ + $x^3$ + $x^4 \rm\ [m]$
